Grammar

Polish is a West Slavic language spoken primarily in Poland and is the native language of the Poles. It is the official language of Poland, but it is also used throughout the world by Polish minorities in other countries. It is one of the official languages of the European Union. [source: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Polish_language]
